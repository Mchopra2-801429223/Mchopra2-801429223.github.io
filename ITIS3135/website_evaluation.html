Website Evaluations

Awwwards

Awwwards recognizes and promotes the skill of web designers, developers, and agencies who create unique digital experiences.

  • Contrast: High contrast between text and background enhances readability.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and layout across pages.
  • Alignment: Well-aligned elements create an organized and professional look.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped effectively for ease of navigation.

Arngren

Arngren is an online retailer offering a variety of gadgets and electronics, but suffers from poor design choices.

  • Contrast: Cluttered layout and overlapping elements reduce readability.
  • Repetition: Lack of consistency in fonts and colors creates confusion.
  • Alignment: Poor alignment of images and text results in a chaotic look.
  • Proximity: Unrelated items are placed too close together, making navigation difficult.
